"2048" is a captivating and addictive puzzle game where the objective is to slide numbered tiles on a 4x4 grid to combine them and create a tile with the number 2048. While easy to grasp, mastering it requires strategic thinking and foresight, challenging your mind to beat your high score.

Gameplay starts with '2' or '4' tiles. Using arrow keys, move all tiles; identical tiles merge to double their value. A new tile appears after each move. The game ends when the board is full with no possible moves. Plan carefully to create merge chains and avoid filling the board.

Controls:
- Arrow Keys: Move tiles (Up, Down, Left, Right)

Special Features:
- AI Assistance: "Auto-run" for optimal strategies, "Hint" for next moves.
- Classic gameplay: Addictive 2048 experience.
- Score tracking: Improve and compete.
- Minimalist design: Focus on the puzzle.
