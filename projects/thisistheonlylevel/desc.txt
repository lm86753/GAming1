"This Is The Only Level" is a deceptively simple yet highly ingenious puzzle-platformer that plays on player expectations. Each "stage" of the game presents a new rule or twist that alters how the player character (often a small elephant) or the environment behaves, forcing players to rethink their approach to the same physical space. It's a game that rewards creative problem-solving and an open mind, proving that simplicity can hide profound depth and endless fun.
