Game Title: Monkey Mart

Description:
Monkey Mart is a charming and addictive management simulation game where players help a cute monkey run a bustling supermarket. The objective is to grow and manage the monkey's business by cultivating crops, producing goods, and selling them to a steady stream of animal customers. Players will harvest bananas, corn, and other produce, process them into items like jams or bread, and then stock the shelves. As the mart grows, new products, upgrades, and employees can be unlocked to increase efficiency and profits. The game focuses on resource management, strategic planning, and keeping customers happy in a lively, animated environment.

Controls:
- Movement: Typically uses W, A, S, D keys or Arrow keys to move the monkey character around the mart.
- Interaction: Usually automatic when the monkey is near an item to pick up, plant, or stock. Some versions might use the mouse for clicking on upgrades or menu navigation.
- Menu Navigation: Mouse clicks for selecting upgrades, hiring staff, and managing inventory.

Special Features and Mechanics:
- Farm-to-Store Workflow: Players manage the entire process from growing ingredients to selling finished products.
- Upgradable Facilities: Invest in new machinery, larger plots, and faster production lines.
- Employee Management: Hire other animals to assist with tasks like harvesting, stocking, and checkout, allowing for automation and expansion.
- Customer Satisfaction: Keeping shelves stocked and efficiently serving customers is key to earning more coins.
- Progression System: Unlock new items, areas, and challenges as the mart expands and profits grow.
- Cute and Colorful Graphics: A visually appealing art style that makes the management tasks enjoyable.
