Bullet Force is a fast-paced first-person shooter that delivers classic military-style combat in both multiplayer and offline modes. Players engage in intense gunfights across a variety of maps, using realistic weapons and tactical movement. The main objective depends on the mode—eliminate opponents, complete objectives, or outscore the enemy team—while staying alive and managing resources.

Gameplay emphasizes smooth gunplay, map awareness, and reflexes. Players can customize their loadouts with different rifles, pistols, attachments, and perks, allowing for flexible playstyles such as aggressive rushing or careful positioning. The game includes popular modes like Team Deathmatch, Free-For-All, Conquest, and Gun Game. Killstreaks and perks reward skilled play, encouraging players to stay active and strategic during matches.

Visually, Bullet Force features realistic 3D environments, detailed weapon models, and fluid animations. Controls are responsive, making it accessible to new players while still offering depth for competitive ones. The ability to play offline against bots also makes it a strong choice for practice or solo play.

Controls (Keyboard & Mouse)

W, A, S, D – Move

Mouse Movement – Look around / aim

Mouse Left Click – Shoot

Mouse Right Click – Aim down sights

R – Reload

Spacebar – Jump

Shift – Sprint

C – Crouch

F – Use / interact

1–5 – Switch weapons

G – Throw grenade

Esc – Pause menu

Special Features & Mechanics

Online multiplayer and offline bot modes

Customizable weapons and attachments

Multiple game modes and maps

Smooth, skill-based FPS combat

Killstreaks and perk system

Bullet Force offers a polished FPS experience with flexible play options, making it popular among fans of competitive and casual shooter gameplay alike.
