Game Title: Yohoho.io

Description:
Yohoho.io is a thrilling multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game with a pirate theme, where players compete to become the richest and most powerful pirate on an island. The objective is to collect gold, defeat other players, and avoid being eliminated by environmental hazards or stronger opponents. Players start as small pirates and can grow in size and power by accumulating gold. The core gameplay involves strategic movement, attacking other players to steal their loot, and utilizing power-ups or special abilities. It's a fast-paced, competitive .io game designed for quick, action-packed sessions.

Controls:
- Movement: Typically controlled by the mouse (moving the cursor dictates direction) or WASD/Arrow keys for movement.
- Attack: Left-click or Spacebar to attack nearby enemies.
- Dash/Special Ability: Right-click or Shift key for a quick dash or to activate a special skill, often consuming some gold or a cooldown.

Special Features and Mechanics:
- .io Gameplay: Drop-in, drop-out multiplayer action with a focus on quick, competitive rounds.
- Size and Power Progression: Grow larger and stronger by collecting gold and defeating foes.
- Pirate Theme: Engaging graphics and sound design set in a swashbuckling pirate world.
- Treasure Hunting: Collect gold coins scattered around the map and from defeated enemies.
- Strategic Combat: Balance aggressive attacks with evasive maneuvers and resource management.
- Various Characters/Skins: Unlock different pirate characters or cosmetic skins for personalization.
